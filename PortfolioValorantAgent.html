Valorant Fan Agent 27

Genre: Character Design Document
Software: Google Docs
Project Length: 3 days

As training to become a better game designer, I created this document to practice designing a custom Valorant Agent. I came up with all the abilities after a brainstorming session. I then iterated after receiving feedback from friends who play Valorant at various levels.

Audio Cues

  • When the EMP blast is charging up, it will make a whirring sound that can be heard by nearby players, even through walls.
  • When the EMP goes off it will make a powering down sound, whether there is an enemy or ability in the range or not
  • When the EMP goes off, if there is at least one player or ability in the range of the EMP a hitmarker sound will play. This sound does not change whether it is an enemy or ability. This sound does not change whether it is one item or many.

    This gives players in range the chance to move and the agents a chance to move their abilities.

Design Intent

  • Playstyle Goals:
    • 27 was designed to be a penetrating agent, countering the strength of Sentinels like Cypher’s ability to shut down an entire site by themselves.
    • 27 is strong on attack, able to locate which sites have agent activity via placed abilities.
    • 27 is weaker on defense, however his utility gives teams a strong reason to play for retake and his Combat Stim would give defending sites an advantage in gunfights.
    • Given information of placed abilities, players can decide whether to push a site or abandon it, but the information of abilities can assist a push by avoiding cameras, tripwires, turrets, orbs, etc.
  • Synergy
      The agent's abilities are designed to work against agents such as Cypher or Killjoy who are able to shutdown sites via their placed abilities.

Post-Mortem

  • What went right:
    • Agent 27 seems like he would be a fun agent to play according to friends and fellow Valorant players.
    • Agent 27 had a purpose and was able to complete his purpose.
    • Agent 27 followed similar patterns to in-game Initiators, I believe if you looked at his abilities it would be obvious he is an Initiator.
  • What went wrong:
    • Agent 27 is weak on defense, while he could be a strong attacker the agent seem very one-sided
    • Agent 27 targets certain agents, putting some agents at a large disadvantage like Cypher or Viper. However, 27 also barely touches some agents, like Phoenix or Jett.
    • Agent 27 is very similar to Kayo, if introduced into the game he would essentially make Kayo useless. This would need balancing to make both agents necessary.

Over the course of this project I have grown interested in seeing how some of these abilities would react to the Valorant ecosystem. While I understand this character will likely never see the light of day, seeing some of these abilities created in some form with another agent would be interesting. Due to my limited contacts with professionals or high level play (above Immortal rank) it is unknown how a professional player would react or use this character. Still, this character will always be a Valorant agent. At least in my head-cannon.
