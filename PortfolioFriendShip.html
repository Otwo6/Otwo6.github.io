Genre: 2D Co-Op Puzzle

Once upon a time, there was a great captain and his loyal crew. One day, they got lost at sea and a witch appeared. Without mercy, she killed the captain's crew. Leaving him stranded on an island, the captain stumbles upon an amulet containing the soul of his first mate. Together, the captain and his ghostly first mate set out to find the witch's amulet, which contains the rest of the captain's crew.
