A Little About Me

I am a third-year Computer Science student currently attending California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, based in Corona, California.

My passion is creating living, breathing worlds in video games. I love the intense nature of game jams, constantly refining my craft as a programmer, game designer and team leader.

I truly love working on games, crafting stories and experiences. This love for games led to me learning programming and game development, which led to an equal passion for creating them.

Right now, I am either studying, playing games, working out, or saving up money to go to Disneyland again.
