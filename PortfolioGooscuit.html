My Bondling - Gooscuit

Genre: Pet-Simulation
Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Team Size: 2

From another world, little pets adorably named "Bondlings" fell from the sky and humans immediately took a liking. One of these pets is named a Gooscuit, a mix between a snail and a dog. These Gooscuits can be a real handful to take care of when they are young, experience the life of a Gooscuit owner for a short time.

Programmed

  • Gooscuit AI Hunger and Bathroom Needs
  • Gooscuit AI Movement
  • Pick Up Interaction System
  • Game Timer

Showcase

  • A key mechanic to taking care of your Gooscuit is the interaction system, letting you pick up toys, feed and clean up for your Bondling, and opening and closing doors.
  • This created an easy to understand system that could be further expanded on with different puzzles, hiding traps, clues, doors, and more.
  • While playing your Gooscuit will move around your house.
  • Your Gooscuit will also poop, walk, and sleep as it sees fit. Keep your house clean and his belly full!
