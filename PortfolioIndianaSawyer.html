Indiana Sawyer

Genre: 2D Puzzle-Platformer
Engine: Unity
Team Size: 1

Indiana Sawyer is a brave explorer and adventurer, known for his daring expeditions into the most dangerous places. He hears from a fellow explorers about a jewel that everyone else is too frightened to attempt to obtain, the Blood Banana. The banana eaten by the Mayan Monarch Ut Chanal as he died, Sawyer enters to a pyramid for what could be his last time.
