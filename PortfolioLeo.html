Leo Saves the Swamp

Genre: 3D Foddian-Platformer
Engine: Unreal Engine 4
Team Size: 3

One day a small frog named Leo is gazing at the clouds till a large object burst through the sky, and lands in the forest. Upon finding it, the meteor opens, releasing hoards of dangerous flies that kill plants they touch. Now with an empty stomach and even emptier mind, our hero must rid his home of this alien menace!
